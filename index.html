Driving School Terms & Conditions

1. Training Schedule Practical classes will be conducted according to the schedule given by the driving school.
2. Punctuality Students must arrive on time for their driving lessons. If student late more than 15 minutes class will cancel for that day.
3. Valid Documents Students must have a valid Learner’s License (LL) before starting practical driving training.
4. Class Cancellation If a student wants to cancel or change a class, they must inform the driving school at least 1 hours in advance. You are permitted to cancel or reschedule up to (3) classes during the course without any penalty. Any class cancel beyond this limit will be count as per of the total no of classes in the selected package.
5. Payment Policy Full course fees must be paid before . Or two-part payment facility is available. At the time of admission, 75% of the total course fee must be paid. The remaining 25% must be paid after the completion of the 7th class as per the school policy.
6. Refund Policy Course fees once paid are non-refundable under normal circumstances.
7. Safety Rules Students must follow all traffic rules and instructor instructions during training.
8. Damage Responsibility If any damage happens due to intentional negligence, the student may be responsible.
9. Weather / Holiday Policy Classes may be postponed due to bad weather, public holidays, or unavoidable situations.
10. Instructor Decision The instructor’s decision regarding training progress and readiness for the driving test will be final.
11. Driving Test The driving school will guide for the RTO driving test, but passing the test depends on the student’s performance.
12. Time Duration Each practical class will be conducted for the fixed duration decided by the driving school.
13. Rule Changes The driving school reserves the right to modify rules or schedules when necessary.
